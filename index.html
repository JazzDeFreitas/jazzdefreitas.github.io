Games

I make games as a hobby. My games are developed in JavaScript with Phaser, and I publish them online to numerous sites. I ported my latest game, Melody Shuffle, to mobile using Apache Cordova, and published it on the App Store and Google Play Store. My second game, Rhythm Rebuild, was made in 48 hours for the Kennesaw State University Global Game Jam in 2020, in which it was awarded third place.

Melody Shuffle - a musical puzzle game
Rhythm Rebuild - a one button rhythm game
FoxBot - a retro platformer

Along with programming and creating sprite art, I also compose music for my games.
